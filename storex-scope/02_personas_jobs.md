# Personas & Jobs To Be Done

This document defines the **key user personas** and their **jobs to be done** when using Storex.  
Understanding user goals helps us design features that truly solve problems.

---

## 1. Merchant Owner

### Profile
- **Age**: 25-45
- **Business Type**: Small to medium online seller
- **Channels**: Primarily Instagram/Facebook
- **Tech Savvy**: Medium (comfortable with social media, not with complex tools)
- **Team Size**: Solo or 1-3 people
- **Monthly Revenue**: ₮500K - ₮5M

### Main Goal
**Increase sales without hiring more staff**

### Jobs To Be Done

#### Selling Jobs
- **See what to sell**: Understand which products perform best
- **Know which channel works**: Identify where customers come from
- **Convert more conversations**: Turn "just browsing" into orders
- **Respond faster**: Never lose a sale due to slow replies
- **Upsell effectively**: Recommend complementary products

#### Operational Jobs
- **Track orders easily**: Know order status at a glance
- **Manage inventory**: Avoid overselling out-of-stock items
- **Coordinate delivery**: Ensure customers get orders on time
- **Handle payments**: Receive money without complex setup

#### Growth Jobs
- **Monitor AI performance**: Trust that AI is representing brand well
- **Understand customer patterns**: Learn what customers want
- **Scale without overhead**: Grow sales without growing team

### Pain Points
- ❌ Losing sales due to slow response times
- ❌ Spending hours answering the same questions
- ❌ Complicated payment setup (bank accounts, BNPL contracts)
- ❌ Switching between multiple tools (chat, orders, payments)
- ❌ No time to analyze what's working

### Success Metrics
- ✅ Sales increase by 30%+ within first month
- ✅ Response time drops from hours to seconds
- ✅ 5+ hours saved per week
- ✅ Clear visibility into business health

## 2. Staff / Operator

### Profile
- **Role**: Store assistant, customer service rep
- **Relationship**: Works for merchant owner
- **Skills**: Customer service, basic product knowledge
- **Availability**: Part-time or full-time
- **Tech Comfort**: Varies (needs simple interface)

### Main Goal
**Handle edge cases only, focus on fulfillment**

### Jobs To Be Done

#### Customer Service Jobs
- **Step in when AI escalates**: Handle complex questions AI can't answer
- **Resolve complaints**: Deal with unhappy customers
- **Provide personalized service**: Build customer relationships
- **Confirm special requests**: Handle custom orders or modifications

#### Operational Jobs
- **Manage orders & delivery**: Prepare, pack, coordinate shipping
- **Update inventory**: Mark products as in/out of stock
- **Process offline orders**: Create orders from phone calls or in-person sales
- **Handle returns/exchanges**: Process post-sale issues

#### Coordination Jobs
- **Communicate with owner**: Report issues and get guidance
- **Work with AI**: Review AI suggestions, override when needed
- **Train AI**: Help AI learn better responses over time

### Pain Points
- ❌ Overwhelmed by repetitive questions
- ❌ Context switching between chat apps
- ❌ Not knowing if AI already responded to customer
- ❌ Manual data entry for orders

### Success Metrics
- ✅ 80%+ questions handled by AI
- ✅ Focus on fulfillment, not FAQs
- ✅ Clear handoff between AI and human
- ✅ Single tool for all tasks

## 3. End Customer

### Profile
- **Age**: 18-40
- **Platform**: Instagram/Facebook active user
- **Shopping Behavior**: Browses social media, buys on impulse
- **Payment Preference**: Mobile-friendly, BNPL preferred
- **Patience Level**: Low (expects instant responses)

### Main Goal
**Buy fast with confidence**

### Jobs To Be Done

#### Discovery Jobs
- **Ask price, stock, delivery**: Get basic product info instantly
- **Compare options**: See different sizes, colors, variants
- **See social proof**: Read reviews, see other buyers
- **Get recommendations**: Find products that match needs

#### Purchase Jobs
- **Checkout easily**: Complete purchase without leaving conversation
- **Choose payment method**: Pay now or use BNPL
- **Schedule delivery**: Pick delivery time or pickup
- **Get confirmation**: Receive order details immediately

#### Post-Purchase Jobs
- **Track order**: Know where order is
- **Get updates**: Receive delivery notifications
- **Ask questions**: Contact seller if issues arise
- **Leave feedback**: Share experience

### Pain Points
- ❌ Waiting hours (or days) for seller replies
- ❌ Having to ask the same question multiple times
- ❌ Complicated checkout process
- ❌ Uncertainty about delivery time
- ❌ No payment flexibility (BNPL)

### Success Metrics
- ✅ Response within 30 seconds
- ✅ Checkout in < 2 minutes
- ✅ Clear order tracking
- ✅ Payment flexibility (BNPL)

## 4. AI Assistant (Storex AI)

### Profile
- **Role**: Always-on sales representative
- **Availability**: 24/7
- **Learning**: Improves over time
- **Tone**: Friendly, helpful, professional

### Main Goal
**Convert conversations into orders while maintaining trust**

### Jobs To Be Done

#### Customer Engagement Jobs
- **Detect buying intent**: Identify when customer is ready to buy
- **Answer questions instantly**: Provide accurate product information
- **Recommend products**: Suggest items based on customer needs
- **Guide to checkout**: Walk customer through purchase process
- **Build rapport**: Create conversational, natural interaction

#### Merchant Support Jobs
- **Alert on important events**: Notify merchant of urgent matters
- **Provide insights**: Analyze conversation patterns
- **Suggest improvements**: Recommend product or pricing changes
- **Handle routine tasks**: Free up merchant time

#### Learning Jobs
- **Improve over time**: Learn from merchant corrections
- **Adapt tone**: Match store brand voice
- **Optimize timing**: Know when to follow up
- **Recognize patterns**: Identify common questions

### Pain Points
- ❌ Misunderstanding customer intent
- ❌ Lacking product knowledge
- ❌ Sounding too robotic
- ❌ Escalating too often to merchant
- ❌ Token exhaustion (running out of AI energy)

### Success Metrics
- ✅ 20%+ conversation → order conversion
- ✅ 90%+ questions answered without escalation
- ✅ Response time < 30 seconds
- ✅ High customer satisfaction (inferred from tone)

### Emotional Jobs

- In control of operations
- Professional and responsive

**How the user wants to be perceived**
- Successful business owner
- Reliable seller
